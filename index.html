Everything You Need

Built for your crew.

No more checking five different websites. Timed pulls every available tee time near you into one place.

🗺️
Search by Location & Radius
Enter your zip code, set how far you're willing to travel — 5, 10, 20, 50 miles — and see every matching course instantly.
⏰
Time Window Search
Tell us you're free between 1pm and 5pm and we'll only show you courses with tee times that actually fit. No more manual filtering.
💸
Group Pay & Split
One person books, everyone pays their share. Pre-filled Venmo, Apple Pay, and Cash App requests sent to your crew automatically.
🌤️
Hourly Weather Forecast
See the temperature and conditions for every tee time slot. Know before you go whether to bring a jacket or sunscreen.
🍺
Bar & Grill Hours
See if the 19th hole is open before you book. Every course shows their bar and grill hours right on the listing.
📊
Score Tracking
Log your score after every round. Track your history, see your progress, and show your friends who the real golfer is.
